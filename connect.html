How to get a token manually

  1. Go to Graph API Explorer
  2. Select your app from the dropdown
  3. Click "Add a Permission" and enable all 6 below:
pages_show_list pages_read_engagement pages_manage_posts pages_read_user_content read_insights pages_manage_metadata
  1. Click "Generate Access Token"
  2. Approve all permissions in the Facebook popup
  3. Copy the token and paste it above

Your data is safe

Artemis only reads your page data ‚Äî we never post, edit, or delete anything on your pages. Tokens are encrypted at rest and transmitted over HTTPS. You can revoke access at any time from your Facebook settings.
